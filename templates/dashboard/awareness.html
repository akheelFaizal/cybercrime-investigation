Cybersecurity Awareness

Phishing

Be suspicious of emails asking for sensitive information. Check the sender's email address and hover over links before clicking.

Strong Passwords

Use complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) whenever possible.

Public Wi-Fi

Avoid accessing bank accounts or sensitive data on public Wi-Fi networks using a VPN for encryption.

Software Updates

Keep your operating system and antivirus software up to date to protect against the latest threats.
